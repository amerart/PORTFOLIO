Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work correctly, or to improve them, and to provide information to website owners about how visitors use their site.

2. Essential Cookies

Some cookies are strictly necessary for this website to function. These cookies cannot be disabled, as they are required to deliver the service you requested.

3. Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

With your consent, this website uses Google Analytics to collect anonymous statistics about how visitors use the site. No personally identifiable information is collected.

These cookies are only set if you consent to analytics cookies.

We use Axeptio to manage your cookie consent. When you first visit the website, a banner appears allowing you to choose which cookies to accept. Axeptio stores your choice so you are not asked again on subsequent visits.
