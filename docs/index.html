Press & Interviews

English ▼

Q: Can you tell us how you discovered your passion for art?
A: I discovered art in 2017, after I arrived in Paris as an immigrant from Sudan. Life was very difficult, and drawing became my therapy and hope.

Q: How would you describe your artistic style?
A: My work is minimal and graphic, with geometric patterns and a limited color palette: black, red, yellow, and blue.

Q: What inspires you the most?
A: I am inspired by my childhood in Sudan—counting stars, helping my family, and searching for water. These memories shape my abstract patterns.

Q: How do you see the future of your art?
A: I want my art to travel, to connect with people beyond language and borders.
