2. Purpose of This Website

This website is an artist portfolio presenting artworks, exhibitions, press coverage, news and a contact form. It is not a commercial website and does not sell products or services.

3. Data We Collect

We may collect the following data when you visit this website:

  • Anonymous analytics data (pages visited, time spent, referring pages)
  • Device and browser type
  • Approximate geographic location (country/city level, derived from IP address)
  • If you use the contact form: your email address and message content

We do not collect names, phone numbers, or payment information.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To understand how visitors use this website and improve its content (Google Analytics)
  • To respond to messages sent through the contact form

We do not sell, share, or rent your data to third parties.

5. Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google LLC. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous statistical data about website usage. These cookies are only activated if you consent to analytics cookies via the cookie consent banner.

You can opt out at any time by updating your cookie preferences or by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Cookie Consent — Axeptio

This website uses Axeptio to manage cookie preferences. When you visit for the first time, a consent banner appears allowing you to accept or refuse non-essential cookies. Your preferences are saved and can be changed at any time.

See our Cookie Policy for full details.

7. Your GDPR Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right of rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right of erasure — you can request that your data be deleted
  • Right to object — you can object to the processing of your data
  • Right to restriction of processing — you can ask us to restrict how your data is used

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: amer.99.er@gmail.com

8. Data Retention

Contact form messages are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry. Anonymous analytics data is retained by Google according to their data retention settings (default: 26 months).

9. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised.
